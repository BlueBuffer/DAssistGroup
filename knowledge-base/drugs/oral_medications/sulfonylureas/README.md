# Sulfonylureas

Sulfonylureas are oral medications that stimulate insulin secretion from pancreatic beta cells.

They are effective in lowering blood glucose but carry a higher risk of hypoglycemia compared to other oral agents.

## Clinical Use
- Used in Type 2 diabetes
- Often prescribed when metformin alone is insufficient
- Requires careful dose adjustment

## Examples
- Glipizide
- Glyburide
