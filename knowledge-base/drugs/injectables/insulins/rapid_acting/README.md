# Rapid-Acting Insulins

Rapid-acting insulins are used to control post-meal (prandial) blood glucose levels.
They begin working within minutes and are typically administered immediately before meals.

## Clinical Use
- Used in both Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes
- Often combined with long-acting insulin in basal-bolus therapy
- Helps manage blood glucose spikes after meals

## Examples
- Insulin Aspart
- Insulin Lispro
